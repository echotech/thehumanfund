It all started in a WalMart on Black Friday

I was there early to get a doll for my son. I reached for the last one they had, but so did another man. As I rained blows upon him, I realized there had to be another way.

That's when I created:

A FESTIVUS FOR THE REST OF US!

  • Eschew all commercial and religious aspects of Christmas.
  • Purchase a Festivus pole. The pole should be aluminum for the strength to weight ratio.
  • Festivus will be celebrated on December 23rd.
  • Festivus dinner is officially started with the airing of grievances.
  • Festivus ends with the feats of strength.
